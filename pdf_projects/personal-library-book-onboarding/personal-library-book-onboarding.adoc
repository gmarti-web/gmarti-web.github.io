== Introduction

This guide describes how to manage books in the Scottinez library collection on the Libib app.

IMPORTANT: The procedures below require the {url-libib-app}[Libib mobile application].

== Add a new book

Onboard new books into Libib as you get them. Libib offers four different ways to do so; if one way fails, try the next one. See the following flowchart for reference.

.New book process flowchart
image::resources/images/add-new-book.drawio.png[A flowchart showing decisions a user must make when adding a book to a Libib collection.]

=== Scan barcode

.Prepare the barcode
. Remove stickers or other obstructions from the barcode.

.Scan the barcode with the Libib app
. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-plus.png[Libib + button,20,20] and then select *Barcode*.
. Select *Book*.
. Center the book's barcode on your screen using your phone's camera. Libib automatically captures the barcode.
. Select *Add another* to add another book or select *Done* to save your changes.

The book appears in the collection.

=== Enter ISBN

. Find the ISBN 13 or ISBN 10 on the book's copyright page or near the barcode.
+
--
ISBN 13:: An identification number with 13 digits. An ISBN 13 code starts with _978_ or _979_. Books printed after 2007 only have an ISBN 13.
ISBN 10:: An identification number with 10 digits. An ISBN 10 code starts with _0_ or _1_. Books printed before 2007 have both an ISBN 10 and an ISBN 13, but may only show the ISBN 10.
--
+
. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-plus.png[Libib + button,20,20] and then select *Barcode*.
. Select *Book*.
. Enter the ISBN 13 or ISBN 10 in the search bar along the top of the screen and then tap *return*.
. Select *Add another* to add another book or select *Done* to save your changes.

The book appears in the collection.

=== Search by title

. Go to {url-libib}[Libib.com] and log in to the _greagancatalog@protonmail.com_ account.
. In the sidebar menu, select *Add Items*.
. Under _Select Item Type_, choose *Book*.
. Enter the book's name in the search bar under _Search for Books_ and then select *Search*.
. Find the entry that most closely matches the book and then select *Add Item*.
+
--
Check the following attributes to find the closest match:

* Title
* Cover image
* Author
* Publish year
* Number of pages
* Publisher (in parantheses next to number of pages)
--

The book appears in the collection.

=== Create new book

. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-plus.png[Libib + button,20,20] and then select *Manual*.
. Select *Book*.
. Fill out the Manual Entry form and then select *Submit*. The following table describes each field in the form.
+
--
.Manual Entry form
|===
|Field |Description |Required?

s|Title
|The book's name.
|YES

s|Authors (comma separate)
|A comma-separated list of the book's authors.
|YES

s|Description
|The brief description of the book on the inside flap or back cover.
|YES

s|Price
|The book's price.
|NO

s|Publisher
|The book's publisher as listed on the copyright page or spine.
|YES

s|Publish Date (YYYY MM DD)
|The book's publish date or year as listed on the copyright page.
|YES (year only)

s|ISBN 13
|The book's ISBN 13 code.
|NO

s|ISBN 10
|The book's ISBN 10 code.
|NO

s|Pages
|The book's total page count, including backmatter (notes, bibliography, or index).
|YES (except for audiobooks)
|===
--

== Edit an existing book

. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select the book that you want to edit.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-meatballs.PNG[Libib book meatball menu,20,20] and then select *Edit*.
. Make the required changes and then select *Save*.

== Remove a book

. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select the book that you want to remove.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-meatballs.PNG[Libib book meatball menu,20,20] and then select *Delete*.
. Select *Delete* again to confirm.
