== Introduction

This guide describes how to manage books in the Scottinez library collection on the Libib app.

IMPORTANT: The procedures below require the {url-libib}[Libib mobile application].

== Onboard a new book

Onboard new books into Libib as you get them. Libib offers four different ways to do so; if one way fails, try the next one. See the following flowchart for a quick guide on moving through your options.

=== Scan barcode

.Prepare the barcode
. Remove stickers or other obstructions from the barcode.

.Scan the barcode with the Libib app
. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-plus.png[title=Libib + button,20,20] and then select *Barcode*.
. Select *Book*.
. Center the book's barcode on your screen using your phone's camera. Libib automatically captures the barcode.
. Select *Add another* to add another book or select *Done* to save your changes.

The book appears in the collection.

=== Enter ISBN

. Find the ISBN 13 or ISBN 10 on the book's copyright page or near the barcode.
+
--
ISBN 13:: An identification number with 13 digits. An ISBN 13 code starts with _978_ or _979_. Books printed after 2007 only have an ISBN 13.
ISBN 10:: An identification number with 10 digits. An ISBN 10 code starts with _0_ or _1_. Books printed before 2007 have both an ISBN 10 and an ISBN 13, but may only show the ISBN 10.
--
+
. Open the Libib mobile app and select *Scottinez Books*.
. Select image:resources/images/libib-plus.png[title=Libib + button,20,20] and then select *Barcode*.
. Select *Book*.
. Enter the ISBN 13 or ISBN 10 in the search bar along the top of the screen and then tap *return*.
. Select *Add another* to add another book or select *Done* to save your changes.

The book appears in the collection.
